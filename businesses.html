SAHYADRI FOR BUSINESS

Next-Generation Settlement Infrastructure. Global. Instant. Fraud-Proof.

Settlement Confirmed: 1.0s

THE FUTURE OF COMMERCE

Sahyadri provides businesses with a high-speed, low-cost settlement layer that operates 24/7. Stop waiting days for bank clearances. With Sahyadri, your business moves at the speed of the internet. Global payments, settled in 1 second, with zero intermediary risk.

Protection Against Fraud

Say goodbye to chargeback fraud. Once a Sahyadri transaction is confirmed on the Sahyadri Consensus, it is final and irreversible. Your revenue is protected by the same 100% PoW security that powers the entire network.

Choose Your Own Fees

There is no fee to receive Sahyadri, and many wallets let you control how large a fee to pay when spending. Most wallets have reasonable default fees, and higher fees can encourage faster confirmation of your transactions. Fees are unrelated to the amount transferred, so it's possible to send 100,000 Sahyadri for the same fee it costs to send 1 Sahyadri.

Standard Speed 90% lower than banks Express

Instant Liquidity & Settlement

Cash flow is the lifeblood of business. Sahyadri eliminates the 'Pending' status. Payments are settled and available in your wallet instantly, ready to be reinvested or moved to Layer-2 assets like USDS or XAUS.

Global Reach, No Borders

Expand your market to every corner of the planet. Sahyadri allows you to accept payments from anyone, anywhere, without needing a local bank account or worrying about cross-border complexities.

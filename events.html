Sahyadri Events

Sahyadri is an open and decentralized digital network designed to bring together miners, developers, researchers, and community members who are interested in blockchain innovation and decentralized technologies.

Upcoming Events

Currently, there are no official events scheduled.

As the Sahyadri ecosystem grows, we plan to organize a variety of events including developer meetups, mining workshops, technical discussions, and community gatherings. All upcoming events and announcements will be published on this page.

Future Event Categories

Developer Meetups

Technical sessions for developers to explore the Sahyadri protocol, Sahyadri Core, and the Heavy Hash algorithm. These meetups will focus on innovation, development tools, and blockchain research.

Mining Workshops

Educational workshops designed for miners to understand Sahyadri mining infrastructure, hardware optimization, network participation, and reward mechanisms.

Community Meetups

Open community events that allow participants to learn about the Sahyadri ecosystem, discuss ideas, and connect with other members of the decentralized community.

Stay Updated

If you are interested in future Sahyadri events, announcements and updates will be shared here as they become available. We encourage community members, developers, and miners to stay connected and participate in the future growth of the Sahyadri ecosystem.

Sahyadri is built for the community, and future events will help bring developers, miners, and innovators together.

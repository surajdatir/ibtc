Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sahyadri?
Sahyadri is a sovereign Proof-of-Work digital currency network designed for fast, fair, and decentralized value transfer. It focuses on monetary integrity, fixed supply (21 million), and open participation through CPU and GPU mining.
What is Sahyadri used for?
Sahyadri can be used for instant peer-to-peer payments, decentralized applications (dApps), smart contracts, and as a store of value (21M fixed supply). Its low fees and high speed make it ideal for micro-transactions, cross-border settlements, and DeFi applications.
How fast is Sahyadri?
Sahyadri achieves 1-second block finality with over 10,000 transactions per second (TPS). Its parallel architecture allows multiple blocks to be processed simultaneously — no waiting, no congestion.
What consensus mechanism does Sahyadri use?
Sahyadri uses pure Proof-of-Work (PoW) with an ASIC-resistant hashing algorithm optimized for CPU and GPU mining. This ensures true decentralization — anyone with a standard computer can participate in securing the network.
What is a blockchain and why use it?
A blockchain is a distributed digital ledger that records transactions across many computers. It's secure, transparent, and immutable — meaning once data is recorded, it cannot be altered. Sahyadri uses this technology to create a financial system without banks or intermediaries.

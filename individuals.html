SAHYADRI FOR INDIVIDUALS

Own your assets. Secure your future. Experience true financial autonomy.

YOU ARE THE NETWORK

Sahyadri is built for the people. Whether you are a miner, a long-term holder, or someone looking for instant global payments, Sahyadri puts the power of a sovereign financial system directly in your hands. No banks, no permissions—just pure peer-to-peer freedom.

Mobile Payments Made Easy

Sahyadri on mobile allows you to pay with a simple two-step scan-and-pay. There's no need to sign up, swipe your card, type a PIN, or sign anything. All you need is the QR code in your Sahyadri wallet app.

Security and Control

Sahyadri transactions are secured by mathematics and energy. Cryptographic signatures prevent others from spending your money, while Proof-of-Work prevents undoing, rearranging, or losing your transactions. You stay in full control.

Works Everywhere, Anytime

Like email, you don't need recipients to use the same software or wallet. Just their Sahyadri address, and you can transact instantly. The Sahyadri network never sleeps — even on weekends and holidays.

Fast International Payments

Sending Sahyadri across borders is as easy as sending it across the street. No banks making you wait three business days, no extra fees for international transfers, and no limits on minimum or maximum amounts.

Choose Your Own Fees

There's no fee to receive Sahyadri, and many wallets let you control how large a fee to pay when spending. Fees are unrelated to the amount transferred — sending 100,000 Sahyadri costs the same as sending 1 Sahyadri.

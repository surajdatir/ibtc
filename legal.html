Decentralized Protocol

Sahyadri is a decentralized open-source protocol. The network operates through independent nodes that verify transactions and maintain consensus without central control. No individual, organization, or development team can reverse transactions, modify balances, or guarantee network performance. All protocol rules are enforced automatically by the software running on network participants' machines.

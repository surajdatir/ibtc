HOW SAHYADRI WORKS

Decentralized Power. 1-Second Finality. Mathematical Autonomy.

THE BASICS

Getting started with Sahyadri is as simple as sending a message. Once you set up your $CSM Wallet, the network generates your unique cryptographic address. You can share this address to receive payments instantly. Unlike traditional systems, your Sahyadri address is your personal gateway to a global, borderless economy.

THE LEDGER

At the heart of Sahyadri is a shared, immutable public ledger. Unlike linear blockchains, Sahyadri uses a Block-DAG architecture. This allows multiple blocks to be processed in parallel, ensuring that the network remains fast, scalable, and decentralized. Every transaction is chronologically ordered and verified by the entire network.

SAHYADRI MINING

Security is maintained through Mining—a distributed consensus system. Miners use the RandomX algorithm to solve complex puzzles, confirming pending transactions. This process protects the network's neutrality and ensures no single entity can alter the history. In Sahyadri, a new block is secured every 1 second, making it one of the fastest PoW networks in existence.
